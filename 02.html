Project Overview

In March 2024, I had the privilege of conducting a comprehensive cybersecurity awareness training program at Maluti TVET College. This initiative aimed to equip students and faculty with essential digital safety knowledge in an increasingly connected world.

The training focused on practical cybersecurity skills, threat awareness, and best practices for protecting personal and institutional data. Through interactive sessions and real-world scenarios, participants gained valuable insights into modern cyber threats and defense mechanisms.

Training Details

Duration: 2 Weeks Intensive Program
Format: In-person & Virtual Sessions
Audience: IT Students & Faculty
Tools Used: Interactive Presentations, Live Demos, Assessment Tools
