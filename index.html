Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about my services, process, and expertise. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out directly.

What is the core focus of your technical expertise? +

My core expertise lies at the intersection of Full-Stack Development and Scalable System Architecture. I specialize in building robust, performant applications primarily using Python (for backend logic and APIs) and React/TypeScript (for dynamic frontend experiences), and deploying them efficiently using cloud services like AWS or GCP. I focus on code quality and ensuring maintainability.

Are your projects open-source? Where can I view the code? +

Yes, the majority of my key projects are available on my GitHub profile. I believe in transparency and collaborative coding. You can view project source code, commit history, and the detailed READMEs which often include architectural diagrams and setup instructions. (Note: Code for active commercial projects is often restricted to proprietary libraries, but the architectural overviews are provided in the Case Studies.)

What materials do you require to begin discussing a potential hire or contract? +

To provide the most accurate assessment, I request a clear Scope of Work (SOW) or Job Description, details on the primary tech stack, and information regarding the expected architectural challenges and team structure.

Are you currently available for new projects or full-time roles? +

I am selectively open to new opportunities. While my focus is primarily on my business ventures, I consider exciting, high-impact consulting projects or senior architectural roles that align with my expertise in scalable systems and advanced technologies.

What makes your portfolio different from others? +

My portfolio offers a unique blend of technical depth, entrepreneurial drive, and proven social responsibility. Many developers focus solely on code; I demonstrate the ability to architect, code, launch, and manage systems that deliver commercial or educational value, making me a well-rounded and immediately impactful professional.
