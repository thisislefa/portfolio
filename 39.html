Billing & Subscriptions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time directly from the app. Upgrades happen instantly, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
Is there a commitment period?
For monthly plans, there is no commitment; cancel anytime. Yearly plans are billed upfront for a 20% discount, but if you cancel early, the remaining months are not refunded.
How does the Metal card work?
The Metal card is shipped to you via express delivery within 3 days of upgrading. It is contactless enabled and weighs 18g.
