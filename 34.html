Our Mission

Democratizing access to mental wellness for everyone.

Delight was founded on the belief that mental healthcare should be accessible, affordable, and stigma-free. We are bridging the gap between patients and the care they deserve.

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by Dr. Emily Stone and Mark Alen, Delight began as a small community clinic in Seattle. Witnessing the barriers patients faced—long waitlists, high costs, and confusing insurance policies—they set out to build a digital-first solution.

Today, Delight connects thousands of individuals with licensed therapists through a secure, easy-to-use platform. We believe that technology, when paired with human empathy, can solve the mental health crisis.

Our goal isn't just to treat illness, but to foster proactive wellness and resilience in every community we serve.
